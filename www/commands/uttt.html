Ultimate Tic Tac Toe Website

This project was worked on on two occasions. Implementing the website with a trainable genetic algorithm was my final project for the BMS. Then during my bachelor i extended the training with a particle swarm optimization algorithm. So now you can self train bots with different optimization algorithms and compete against them in Ultimate Tic Tac Toe. Try it out here or play a round below. There are rumors of people beating the hard level, but neither did i do it myself nor did i witnessed anyone else do it anytime.
